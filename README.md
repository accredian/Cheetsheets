## 🎯 Why This Repo?

In the fast-paced world of AI and CS, leaders don't always have time to read 50-page whitepapers. These cheatsheets provide:

  * **Instant Clarity:** Complex concepts (like LLM fine-tuning or Neural Architectures) condensed into single pages.
  * **Leadership Context:** Not just syntax, but **Strategic KPIs** and **Decision Matrixes**.
  * **Zero Fluff:** Hard facts, essential formulas, and industry best practices.
